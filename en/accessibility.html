Our commitment

SHEKEL – Community Integration for People with Disabilities works to ensure that every person, including people with disabilities, can use its services independently, with dignity, safely and equally. We invest significant effort and resources in making this website and our services accessible, out of the belief that accessibility is a basic right.

This website was made accessible in accordance with the Israeli Equal Rights for Persons with Disabilities Regulations (Service Accessibility Adjustments), 2013, and the guidelines of the international WCAG 2.1 standard.

Accessibility level

The website meets the requirements of conformance level AA of WCAG 2.1. Among other things, we implemented:

  • Valid semantic structure, hierarchical headings and consistent navigation across all pages.
  • Full keyboard navigation and operation, with clear focus indicators.
  • Alternative text for meaningful images and icons.
  • Sufficient color contrast between text and background.
  • Support for screen readers and assistive technologies.
  • A "skip to main content" link and respect for the system's "reduced motion" preference.

Accessibility tools

Every page includes an accessibility menu (the floating button in the corner of the screen) that lets you personalize the display. Your preferences are saved for future visits:

  • High contrast and grayscale modes.
  • Highlighting links and headings.
  • A readable font and increased line height & text spacing.
  • Text alignment, hiding images and stopping animations.
  • An enlarged cursor.
Please note: if you can't see the accessibility button, make sure JavaScript is enabled in your browser. We're happy to help in any other way via the details below.

Known limitations

Despite our efforts to make all pages and components accessible, certain parts — mainly third-party content or older documents — may not yet be fully accessible. We work continuously to identify and fix such issues. If you come across content that isn't accessible to you, please let us know and we'll address it promptly.

Physical & service accessibility

SHEKEL's buildings and the services provided in them are accessible to people with disabilities, including accessible parking, access routes, accessible restrooms and service adjustments. As SHEKEL operates many settings across the country, we recommend checking the specific accessibility arrangements of a given setting in advance using its contact details.

Feedback & reporting

If you encounter a problem, a barrier or inaccessible content — or have a suggestion for improvement — we'd love to hear from you. Please describe the issue, the page it appeared on, and the browser and assistive technology you used, so we can address it quickly and effectively.
