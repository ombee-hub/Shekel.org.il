Who we are

SHEKEL is one of Israel's leading organizations in the community integration of people with disabilities — out of a belief that every person belongs at the heart of the community.

Our vision

An inclusive society where everyone has a place, a voice and a future

For decades SHEKEL has led the community-integration approach: instead of isolating people with disabilities in institutions, we connect them to the community — to home, work, study and leisure. We accompany people with physical, intellectual, emotional and sensory disabilities throughout life.

We act with deep respect for every person, across all of Israel's populations — Jews and Arabs, religious and secular — believing that a society is measured by how it cares for its most vulnerable members.

Our values
