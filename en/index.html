Who we are

An organization that believes everyone belongs at the heart of the community

For decades SHEKEL has led the community-integration approach in Israel: instead of isolating — we connect. We accompany people with physical, intellectual, emotional and sensory disabilities throughout life, building with them a path to independent, full lives.

  • ✓ Personal guidance throughout life
  • ✓ Professionalism, innovation and measurable results
  • ✓ Full transparency and public accountability

Areas of Activity

Seven circles of support and guidance

Broad, personally tailored support — from home and work to leisure, therapy and accessibility.

Community Housing

Some 180 housing settings across Israel, home to more than 500 residents — based on a vision of independent living in the heart of the neighborhood.

Learn more

Rehabilitative Employment

Employment settings for every functioning level — from sheltered workshops to placement in the open market, with professional support throughout.

Learn more

Emotional Care

Clinical centers providing psychological therapy, neuropsychological assessment and emotional support for people with disabilities and their families.

Learn more

Children & Youth

Since 1992 — educational settings and therapeutic afternoon programs for more than 265 children, in Jerusalem and Petah Tikva.

Learn more

Service, Education & Leisure

Integration in military and national service, education tracks, and over 1,500 participants in clubs, sports and cultural activities.

Learn more

Accessibility

The national accessibility center, active since 1998, guiding organizations and public bodies on physical and service accessibility, per law and regulations.

Learn more

Shekel Worldwide

Training and courses for professionals from around the world, bringing SHEKEL's unique knowledge and experience to the international community.

Learn more
