Seven circles of support and guidance

SHEKEL accompanies people with disabilities throughout life — from home and work to therapy, education, leisure and accessibility. Every solution is personally tailored.

01 · Community Housing

A home, not an institution

SHEKEL operates one of the largest community-housing networks in Israel — some 180 housing settings nationwide, home to more than 500 residents.

Settings are tailored to different functioning levels, from apartments in the community to hostels, based on a vision of independent living, privacy and dignity — in the heart of the neighborhood, not outside it.

02 · Rehabilitative Employment

Work is dignity and meaning

A wide range of employment settings tailored to every functioning level — from sheltered workshops and rehabilitative employment centers to placement and integration in the open market.

SHEKEL professionals accompany the way throughout, believing in every person's ability to contribute, earn and grow.

03 · Emotional Care

Professional emotional support

SHEKEL's clinical centers provide psychological therapy, neuropsychological assessment and emotional support for people with disabilities and their families.

Care is given with a tailored, respectful and professional approach, helping people cope, grow and improve their quality of life.

04 · Children & Youth

Opening a future from an early age

Since 1992 SHEKEL has run educational settings and therapeutic afternoon programs for more than 265 children, in Jerusalem and Petah Tikva.

The settings combine education, therapy and empowerment, giving children and youth tools to grow — and giving families peace of mind and support.

05 · Service, Education & Leisure

Full lives, in every color

Integrating people with disabilities into meaningful military and national service, alongside education and training tracks.

And more than 1,500 participants enjoy a rich variety of clubs, sports activities and cultural events — because everyone deserves to enjoy, create and grow.

06 · Accessibility

Making the world equal

SHEKEL's national accessibility center has been active since 1998, guiding organizations, businesses and public bodies through physical and service accessibility, in line with the law and regulations.

Our experts provide consulting, accessibility surveys, training and the accessibility of websites and documents — accumulating unique knowledge that benefits all of society.

07 · Shekel Worldwide

Leading knowledge on the international stage

SHEKEL delivers training and courses to professionals from around the world, sharing the unique knowledge and experience it has gained in community integration.

In this way the Israeli model becomes a source of inspiration and a model to follow within the international professional community.
