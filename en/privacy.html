Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. Here you'll find an explanation of the information we collect on the site, how we protect it, and what your rights are.

General

SHEKEL – Community Integration for People with Disabilities (the "Association") respects the privacy of users of the website. This document explains how we collect, use and protect information provided on the site. Browsing and using the site constitute consent to the terms set out in this policy.

What information is collected

The site may collect two types of information:

  • Information you provide voluntarily — for example when filling out a contact form, subscribing to updates or making a donation (name, email, phone, etc.).
  • Statistical and anonymous information — usage and browsing data collected in aggregate to improve the site and the service.

Use of information

Collected information is used only for the following purposes:

  • Responding to inquiries and requests.
  • Processing donations and issuing receipts.
  • Sending updates and content, subject to your consent.
  • Improving the user experience and tailoring site content.

We do not share personal information with third parties unless required by law or to provide the service you requested.

Cookies

The site may use cookies for its proper operation, to save preferences (such as accessibility settings) and to produce statistics. You can block or delete cookies through your browser settings, though this may affect some services.

Data security

We take accepted measures to secure the information collected on the site and to prevent unauthorized access to it. However, we cannot guarantee absolute immunity from intrusions, and donations are processed through a secure external payment platform.

Your rights

Under privacy law, every person is entitled to review the information held about them, request its correction or deletion, and unsubscribe from the mailing list at any time. For such requests, please contact us using the details below.
